Landfill - 'De-trashed'

Problem: Americans create nearly 1 tonne of waste every year

Goal: To make landfill exctinct, to reduce waste to 100kg, or 200lbs of waste per year

Data Collection: Implement waste weighing mechanisms on garbage collection trucks

Vizualisation: What would a trash-free world look like? How would it work?

Policy: Charge household per unit of garbage. Packaging pricing into price tags of food. Bottle free/trash free campuses

Behavior change: Getting people to use reusable containers and avoid packaged items

Gamification: 'De-trashed' - monthly message to households about their trash rank, points, improvements, prizes

Evangelism: Colorful and fun movement around the de-trshed lifestyle, test groups, community movement

Technology: API for garbage data, social comparison web app,

Oil - 'Fuel Ninja'

Problem: Americans use 6000 gallons of gasoline per year

Goal: Reduce fuel consumption to 500 gallons per year

Visualisation: How would the world work without oil?

Policy: Fuel tracking from gas stations to vehicles mandatory

Behaviour change: Shift purchasing towars EV. Cycling, care-share, food home delivery

Gamification: Fuel Ninja - monthly letter/email sent to car owners on their fuel consumption with stars, ranks, points, charts. Real-time data billboard on major freeways, color rank sticker on vehicles for fuel efficiency.

Evangelism: 'Dump the pump' campaign, an oil-free life.

Technology: Data from gas station purchases link to VID in database and API. Web app to show fuel consumption by location.
